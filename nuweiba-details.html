Discover the Tranquil Beauty of Nuweiba, Egypt

Escape to the serene shores of Nuweiba, a hidden gem on the Red Sea coast. With its golden sandy beaches, calm turquoise waters, and rugged desert mountains, Nuweiba is a haven for nature lovers and tranquility seekers. Dive or snorkel in vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life, or simply unwind under the warm sun. Experience the charm of local Bedouin culture, savor fresh seafood, and relax in a laid-back, idyllic atmosphere. Nuweiba is the perfect destination for those looking to connect with nature and embrace a peaceful escape.

Trip Highlights

Details

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Location: Nuweiba, Sinai Peninsula, Egypt
  • Activities: Relax on tranquil beaches, snorkel vibrant reefs, experience desert adventures, and connect with local Bedouin culture.
  • Accommodations: Cozy beachfront eco-lodges, luxury resorts, and traditional Bedouin-style camps

Itinerary

Day 1:

Arrival in Nuweiba
  • Arrival and check-in at your beachfront resort or eco-lodge.
  • Welcome drink and time to relax by the beach.
  • Evening free to explore the surroundings.

    • Day 2:

    Beach Activities and Snorkeling
  • Breakfast at the resort.
  • Enjoy snorkeling or diving at nearby coral reefs.
  • Relax on the sandy beaches with scenic mountain views.
  • Optional sunset camel ride along the shore.

    • Day 3:

    Desert Safari and Hiking
  • Early morning breakfast.
  • Desert safari to explore the Colored Canyon or White Canyon.
  • Hiking through breathtaking landscapes.
  • Return to the resort for a relaxing evening.

    • Day 4:

    Bedouin Experience
  • Experience a traditional Bedouin camp with local food and music.
  • Learn about Bedouin culture and crafts.
  • Spend the evening stargazing in the serene desert.

    • Day 5:

    Departure
  • Breakfast and check-out.
  • Transfer back to your preferred destination (Sharm El Sheikh or Cairo).
