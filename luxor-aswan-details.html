Discover Ancient Egypt: A Luxurious Nile River Cruise

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the heart of ancient Egypt. Our luxurious Nile River cruise takes you to the iconic cities of Luxor and Aswan. Explore the magnificent temples of Karnak and Luxor, the Valley of the Kings, and the majestic Philae Temple. Relax on the sundeck, savor delicious cuisine, and experience the magic of the Nile. This is the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of ancient Egypt.

Trip Highlights

Details

  • Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights
  • Location: Luxor and Aswan, Egypt
  • Activities: Temple visits, felucca ride, Nubian village tour, and guided explorations of historic sites
  • Accommodations: 5-star Nile cruise and hotel stays

Itinerary

Day 1:

Arrival in Luxor
  • Meet and assist at the airport or train station.
  • Check-in at your hotel or Nile cruise.
  • Visit Karnak Temple and Luxor Temple.
  • Evening free for leisure.

    • Day 2:

    Luxor West Bank
  • Breakfast at the hotel/cruise.
  • Explore the Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, and the Colossi of Memnon.
  • Return to the cruise for lunch and leisure time.

    • Day 3:

    Edfu and Kom Ombo
  • Sail towards Edfu.
  • Visit the Temple of Horus in Edfu.
  • Continue sailing to Kom Ombo and visit the Temple shared by Sobek and Haroeris.
  • Dinner and overnight onboard.

    • Day 4:

    Aswan Highlights
  • Breakfast onboard.
  • Visit the High Dam, the Unfinished Obelisk, and Philae Temple.
  • Enjoy a felucca ride around Elephantine Island and the Botanical Gardens.
  • Overnight in Aswan.

    • Day 5:

    Nubian Village Tour
  • Optional early morning excursion to Abu Simbel.
  • Visit a traditional Nubian village.
  • Return to the cruise for lunch and relaxation.

    • Day 6:

    Departure
  • Breakfast and check-out.
  • Transfer to Aswan airport or train station.

    Starting from 17,499 EG per person.
