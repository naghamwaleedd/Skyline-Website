Oasis of Serenity: A Siwa Oasis Adventure

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse yourself in the tranquility of the Siwa Oasis. Discover a hidden gem in the heart of the Western Desert, where ancient traditions and stunning landscapes converge. Explore the sacred Mountain of the Dead, relax in natural hot springs, and experience the warm hospitality of the Siwa people. This unique destination offers a truly unforgettable experience.

Trip Highlights

Details

  • Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • Location: Siwa Oasis, Egypt
  • Activities: Historical tours, hot springs, desert safari
  • Accommodations: Luxury eco-lodges

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, Temple of Amun, and Cleopatra's Spring

Day 2: Sand dunes safari and Salt Lakes

Day 3: Explore the Old Town of Siwa and departure
